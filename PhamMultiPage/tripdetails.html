Trip Details

Do you love yoga/surf and traveling?

Join 100 Smiles Project on our Yoga/Surf Voluntourism trip this December in El Tunco, El Salvador.

The trip is December 26 - January 2, 2015

The cost includes breakfast, airport pick-up, yoga, surf and a trip to the waterfalls.

You will be staying at the Los Almendros Hotel. This is a great opportunity to relax, practice yoga, try surfing and volunteer at the orphanage.

Spaces are limited! If you're interested and for more details, please Email Us Today!
