Our Mission

100 Smiles Project is a non-for profit organization that helps orphanages and responds to real need in impoverished coastal communities. We generate at least 100 smiles through surf and volunteer initiatives, dental care, feeding programs, school supplies, project builds and toy donations to children in impoverished areas of El Salvador, Nicaragua and Guatemala.

—Elly Pham, Founder
