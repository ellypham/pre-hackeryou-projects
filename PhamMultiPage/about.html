On a Mission to Make Children Smile

Smiles. One at a time. Doesn’t sound like a lot – but each and every one is priceless to me. Each and every one counts.

100 Smiles – that’s our goal – well to bring at least 100 smiles to children living in orphanages in impoverished coastal communities in countries like El Salvador, Nicaragua and Guatemala.

Providing love and care to children, building confidence among children, construction, toy donation,supporting teacher, school supplies, feeding Programs and more.

If you're interested in making a donation, you can Donate Here.

100% of all donations go directly to projects like:

Dental Clinic

We hold dental hygiene clinics at the orphanage and provide children with brand new toothbrushes, tooth paste and floss.

Surf Camp

We take the kids out to the beach for a fun-filled day of activities, swimming and surf lessons.

Volunteer

Bakery Build

With the bakery almost being complete, they'll need help purchasing plates, utensils, cups, etc.
